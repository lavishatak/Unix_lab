EXCERCISE PROGRAMS ON SED COMMAND:

1.Display the employee details who's designation manager/Manager?
->sed -i "empl[manager/Manager]/p" empl.

2.salary which starts with 2?
->sed -d -f 5 "^2" empl.

3.from line 1 to 5 if any designation is manager replace it with director?
->sed -e 'manager/director/g' empl.
